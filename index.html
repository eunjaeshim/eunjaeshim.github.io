About Me

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am currently an Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Michigan, advised by Professors Alison Narayan and Ji Zhu. Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Michigan in 2024 under the guidance of Professors Paul Zimmerman and Tim Cernak after obtaining my M.Sc and B.S. degrees from UC Santa Barbara and Seoul National University, respectively. My research interest lies in developing practical machine learning strategies for organic synthesis and biocatalysis.
